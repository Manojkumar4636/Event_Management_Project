About us

EVENT DETAILS

[ Date - Jan 8 to 10 ] [ Time - 10AM to 5PM ]
We are the Centre for Cellular and Molecular Platforms, an institution with the core mandate of enabling cutting-edge life science research and innovation.

The Centre for cellular and Molecular Platforms, or C-CAMP, was conceptualised by Department of Biotechnology, Govt. of India in 2009 as an enabler or catalyst of cutting-edge research and innovation in the life sciences. We form part of the Bangalore Life Sciences Cluster (BLiSC). Together with the National Centre for Biological Sciences (NCBS) and the Institute for Stem Cell Science and Regenerative Medicine (inStem), C-CAMP strives to make the BLiSC a centre of excellence in life science research and innovation.
